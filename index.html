Missions

Data Cleaning

After gathering my data, data cleaning is the crucial step to prepare my dataset. Through this preprocess, I can identify and correct errors, inconsistencies and inaccuracies, handle outliers, remove irrelevant information and deal with missing data or non-responses. My purpose is to get a relevant and consistent dataset, serving as a solid foundation for analysis.

Data analysis

I perform exploratory data analysis to identify patterns, trends and actionable insights within the data. I use statistical and analytics techniques to interpret the data and draw meaningful conclusions. Using Data analysis, I can Perform tracking and monitor performance over time, with key performance indicators (KPIs), in order to enhance and suggest recommendations.

Dashboards

Dashboards are an effective way to present my data analysis results, and the best narrative storytelling. They include: Data context, real time or frequent updates, Key performance Indicators, and relevant charts to visualize and track the outcomes easily.
